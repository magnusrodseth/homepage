## Why "Celeritas"?

Let's address the elephant in the room: **Silly project name, I know**. Anyway,
check out the [Celeritas website](https://celeritas.vercel.app/) to find out why
the website is named "Celeritas".

## Motivation

This project was developed whilst attending NTNU's
[Algorithms and Data Structures course](https://www.ntnu.edu/studies/courses/TDT4120#tab=omEmnet).
It was a way for me to put theory in practice, visualizing various sorting
algorithms, graph algorithms and pathfinding algorithms.
