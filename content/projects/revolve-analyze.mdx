Provided **technical solutions** to electrical and mechanical engineers whilst
working in a team, providing a great opportunity to seek valuable and
**constructive feedback** from my peers.

Contributed to a **data analysis** desktop application called Analyze, using **C#** and
**XAML**. Plotted data using the **SciChart** library. [Click here for a demo video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ys4f8toUtiM).

As a user, you can visualize **live data** during live testing of the race car or plot
data from logs stored in the cloud (**Azure PostgreSQL database**) postprocessing.
