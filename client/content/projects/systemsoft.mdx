## Introduction

Developed the official SystemSoft AS website from scratch, using **React**, **TypeScript**, **Next.js**, **Strapi**, **Urql (GraphQL client)**, **PostgreSQL**, **Tailwind CSS**, and hosted on **DigitalOcean**. Worked with 1 other developer.

Here's some inline code: `tsconfig.json`, `const hello = () => "World!"`.

## Subheading

The website allows employees of SystemSoft AS to access a Strapi backend in order to **easily, independently,
and continuously** update the company's portfolio served on the Next.js frontend. [Click here to visit the
website](https://www.systemsoft.no/).
